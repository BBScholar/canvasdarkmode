FAQ


How do I report an issue I am having with the extension?

Click the extension's icon in your toolbar, and scroll down to "Report problem via". Then select a medium to report your issue.

The extension is preventing me from being able to use _________. What do I do?

First, report the issue using the steps above. Until the bug is fixed, you can disable to extension temporarily to get the broken feature back.

What browsers is the extension available on?

Dark Mode for Canvas is supported on Chrome, Firefox, & Opera. Browsers such as Brave and Yandex may also work, but are not officially supported.
