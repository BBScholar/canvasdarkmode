FAQ


How do I report an issue I am having with the extension?

A: Click the extension's icon in your toolbar, and scroll down to "Report problem via". Then select a medium to report your issue.

The extension is preventing me from being able to use _____. What do I do?

A: First, report the issue using the steps above. Until the bug is fixed, you can disable to extension temporarily to get the broken feature back.

Does the extension work on other browsers besides Chrome?

A: Dark Mode for Canvas is developed and tested on Google Chrome, but it should work with browsers such as Brave and Yandex. There is also a Firefox Edition, available through Firefox Addons. A Safari extension is in the works as well.
